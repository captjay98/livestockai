# Product Overview

## Product Purpose

LivestockAI is a comprehensive livestock management platform designed for diverse farming operations. It provides offline-first architecture with growth forecasting, financial tracking, and species-specific management tools, addressing the unique challenges of rural farming environments with unreliable internet connectivity.

The platform empowers farmers with data-driven insights for optimizing livestock production, managing costs, and maximizing profitability while maintaining detailed records for regulatory compliance.

## Target Users

### Primary Users

- **Small to medium-scale livestock farmers** - Managing diverse operations from poultry to cattle
- **Specialized farmers** - Operating focused farms (broilers, catfish, cattle, goats, sheep, bees)
- **Mixed livestock operations** - Farmers managing multiple species simultaneously

### Secondary Users

- **Farm managers** - Overseeing day-to-day operations and reporting to farm owners
- **Agricultural consultants** - Providing advisory services and performance analysis
- **Veterinarians** - Monitoring livestock health and treatment protocols

### Tertiary Users

- **Agricultural cooperatives** - Managing multiple member farms and aggregating data
- **Extension services** - Supporting farmers with best practices and training
- **Financial institutions** - Assessing farm performance for lending decisions

## Key Features

### Core Functionality

- Multi-species livestock tracking with extensible module system (currently: Broilers, Catfish; planned: Cattle, Goats, Sheep, Bees)
- Batch management with lifecycle tracking from acquisition to sale
- Growth forecasting using industry-standard growth curves
- Mortality tracking with cause analysis and impact assessment

### Financial Management

- Feed cost tracking with conversion ratio analysis
- Sales revenue management with customer invoicing
- Expense tracking across multiple categories
- Profit/loss analysis with trend reporting
- Nigerian Naira currency support with localized formatting

### Data Management

- Offline-first architecture with automatic synchronization
- Comprehensive audit logs for accountability and compliance
- Data export capabilities for external analysis
- Backup and restore functionality

### User Experience

- Progressive Web App (PWA) for mobile and desktop installation
- Responsive design optimized for smartphones and tablets
- Intuitive interface designed for users with varying technical skills
- Multi-language support potential

## User Journey

### Typical Daily Workflow

1. **Morning Check**: Review overnight mortality, feed consumption, and alerts
2. **Data Entry**: Record daily observations, weights, and feed distribution
3. **Analysis**: Review growth trends and financial performance
4. **Planning**: Adjust feeding schedules and plan upcoming activities
5. **Reporting**: Generate reports for stakeholders and regulatory compliance

### Batch Lifecycle Management

1. **Setup**: Create new batch with species, quantity, and target dates
2. **Monitoring**: Daily tracking of growth, mortality, and feed consumption
3. **Analysis**: Regular weight sampling and growth curve comparison
4. **Optimization**: Adjust feeding and management based on performance data
5. **Harvest**: Plan and execute sales based on market conditions and growth targets
