## ðŸ’¡ What You Should Do (Production Tips)

### 1. The "Side-by-Side" Visual

- Split the screen: Show the **Spec file** on the left and the **Working UI/Feature** on the right.
- _Why_: This proves Kiro's direct impact on the final product.

### 2. Live Terminal (Overlay)

- Pop up a terminal window briefly showing `kiro task ...` or `kiro chat`.
- _Why_: Judges love seeing the tool _actually in use_ rather than just talking about it.

### 3. Show "Intelligence"

- Don't just show a CRUD form. Show the **Feed Formulation** or **Growth Projections**.
- _Explain_: "The agent analyzed the species' growth curve and implemented this projection logic in one pass."

### 4. Language Swap

- Switch the app from English to a local language (e.g., Swahili or French) mid-demo.
- _Why_: It highlights "Application Quality" and the ease of handling complexity with agents.
