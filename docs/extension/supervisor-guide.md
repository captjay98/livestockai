# Supervisor Guide - Multi-District Oversight

## Overview

As a supervisor, you oversee multiple districts and monitor regional farm health trends. This guide covers the supervisor dashboard, regional analytics, and managing extension workers.

## Supervisor Dashboard

### What You See

- **District Cards**: One card per supervised district
- **Aggregated Stats**: Total farms, health distribution, active alerts
- **Regional Trends**: Mortality trends across all districts
- **Drill-Down**: Click any district to view detailed farm list

### District Card Information

Each card shows:

- District name
- Total farms
- Health status distribution (green/amber/red)
- Active outbreak alerts
- Number of extension workers

### Regional Trends Chart

- Mortality rates over time
- Comparison across districts
- Identify problem areas quickly
- Export data for presentations

## Managing Districts

### Your Responsibilities

- Monitor overall district health
- Identify districts needing attention
- Coordinate outbreak responses
- Review extension worker activity

### Drill-Down Workflow

1. Identify district with issues (red status, high alerts)
2. Click "View District" to see farm list
3. Review individual farm health
4. Contact extension workers for updates
5. Escalate critical issues to admins

## Outbreak Management

### Regional Outbreak View

- See all active alerts across districts
- Prioritize by severity (critical first)
- Track resolution progress
- Coordinate multi-district responses

### When to Escalate

- Critical alerts affecting 5+ farms
- Outbreaks spreading across districts
- Unusual mortality patterns
- Suspected disease outbreaks

## Common Questions

### Q: How do I become a supervisor?

**A:** Admins assign the supervisor flag to your user account for specific districts.

### Q: Can I access farms directly?

**A:** Yes, you have the same access as extension workers in your supervised districts.

### Q: What if an extension worker isn't performing?

**A:** Document issues and escalate to admin for reassignment or training.

### Q: How do I add more districts?

**A:** Contact your admin to request additional district assignments.

## Troubleshooting

### Dashboard not loading

- Check internet connection
- Verify you have supervisor permissions
- Contact admin if issue persists

### Missing district data

- Ensure extension workers have active access grants
- Check that farms are properly assigned to districts
- Verify data sync is complete
