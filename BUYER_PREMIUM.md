# Buyer Premium Features & Monetization Strategy

## Overview

The offline marketplace connects farmers (sellers) with buyers (restaurants, retailers, wholesalers, individuals). While farmers pay for listing features, buyers can pay for enhanced purchasing tools and priority access.

## Buyer Protection & Trust

### 1. Dispute Resolution

```
Dispute Process:
1. Buyer reports issue (quality, delivery, etc.)
2. Platform mediates between buyer and seller
3. Evidence collection (photos, messages, contracts)
4. Resolution options:
   - Partial refund
   - Full refund
   - Replacement
   - Credit for future purchase
5. Decision within 7 days (Enterprise: 3 days)
```

### 2. Verified Buyer Badge

```
Requirements:
- Business registration (for businesses)
- ID verification (for individuals)
- 3+ successful purchases
- No disputes in last 6 months
- Payment history review

Benefits:
- Trust signal for sellers
- Priority response from sellers
- Access to exclusive listings
- Higher credit limits
- Faster dispute resolution
```

### 3. Purchase Insurance (Add-on)

```
Coverage:
- Non-delivery: Full refund
- Quality issues: Partial/full refund
- Fraud protection: Full refund + compensation

Cost: 2% of transaction value
Example: $500 purchase = $10 insurance

Claims:
- File claim within 48 hours of delivery
- Provide evidence (photos, videos)
- Decision within 7 days
- Payout within 14 days
```
